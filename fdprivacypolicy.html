Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 09, 2026

1. Introduction

    1.1 "Fancy Drama" (referred to as "the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a free short-form drama platform supported by advertising. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is used to provide our free streaming service.

    1.2 By using Fancy Drama, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. As an ad-supported App, this policy is essential for you to understand how we and our partners personalize your experience.

    1.3 For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: fancydrama.feedback@outlook.com.

    1.4 The App is intended for users aged 13 and older (or the minimum age required by law in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the required age without parental consent.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

    2.1 Advertising and Device Identifiers: Since Fancy Drama is a free, ad-supported service, we collect certain identifiers to serve relevant ads:

    (i) Google Advertising ID (GAID): This allows us and our ad partners to provide personalized advertisements and track ad conversions.

    (ii) Device Technical Data: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, language settings, and IP address. This helps us optimize video rendering and ensure the best playback quality for your specific hardware.

    2.2 Usage Analytics: To improve our content library, we collect anonymous data regarding:

    - Which drama series or episodes are watched most frequently.

    - App performance metrics and crash reports to identify and fix technical bugs.

    2.3 Permissions We Request:

    - Internet Access: Required to stream video content and load advertisements.

    - Network State: Used to detect your connection type (Wi-Fi/Cellular) to adjust video bitrate and prevent unexpected data charges.

    - Notifications: Used to notify you of new episode releases or trending dramas. You may opt-out via system settings at any time.

3. Third-Party Advertising and Analytics

    3.1 Ad-Supported Model: Our service is provided at no cost to you, funded by advertisements. Third-party ad networks may use their own cookies or web beacons to collect information about your activities within the App to provide targeted interest-based ads.

    3.2 Third-Party SDKs: We use standard industry services (such as Google AdMob, AppLovin, or Unity Ads) to facilitate ad delivery. These services handle your data according to their own privacy policies.

    3.3 Your Choices: You can reset or limit your Advertising ID through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads). This will limit the personalization of the ads you see, but will not reduce the number of ads displayed.

4. Data Security and Retention

    4.1 Security: We implement industry-standard encryption for any data transmitted (such as analytics or error reports).

    4.2 Data Retention: We only retain collected technical data as long as necessary to provide the service and improve App performance. Since we do not require account registration for viewing, we do not store personal profiles unless you voluntarily provide contact info for support.

5. User Rights

    5.1 Access and Deletion: You have the right to request information about the data we collect. Since most of our data is linked to your device ID rather than a personal identity, clearing your App cache or uninstalling the App will effectively remove local data.

    5.2 GDPR/CCPA: For users in relevant jurisdictions, we respect your rights to data portability, restriction of processing, and the right to be forgotten.

6. Changes to This Policy

    6.1 We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our ad-supported model or for regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
